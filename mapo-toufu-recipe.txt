Ingredients:
 - toufu
 - spicy source
 - ground beef
 - green onion
 - garlic
 - soy source
 - salt

Directions:
  1. Cut toufu in small cuts and use hot water to filter once 
  2. preheat the oil in pan, and put green onion, garlic and stir fried until small comes out
  3. put ground beef in and then toufu
  4. put soy source and salt in the end
